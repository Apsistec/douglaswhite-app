His career path led him to more advanced technical sales positions where he worked with C-level management and business leaders as a software sales representative & ultimately as a IT services consultant. Thus, he readily understands how to communicate with clients to determine their expectations and he knows how to deliver on them. Consistently observing his clients' desire and need for customized software solutions while also learning about the mass potential that newer coding paradigms offer, Douglas realized that his future demanded that he learn to code if he was going to give clients what they truly needed.

Around three years ago, Douglas was presented with the opportunity to learn JavaScript while on the job. So, he jumped at the chance and has never looked back. His compulsion to understand how all things work ensures that he remains well versed on technologies at the forefront of the industry. Douglas works well on his own, but he also works efficiently as part of a team. Results driven, he is motivated by feelings of accomplishment such as what is felt upon successfully implementing software that turns complex business processes into easy to use, visually appealing solutions that enable a client to achieve the desired impact in their market. He has every intention to continue in that success.
