About Douglas A brief look into My IT career

Douglas White is a Web Application Developer who utilizes JavaScript web frameworks and libraries to design and develop apps that look great on all mobile devices and platforms by utilizing base themes that adapt to each platform. He produces fully featured solutions for clients that enables rapid prototype design while still offering complex features. Using these modern web frameworks also texts to better market penetration and less churn due in part to auto scaling infrastructure. Caching data on a global content delivery network improves app responsiveness and reduces operational expenses. Furthermore, the apps can be shipped to the app stores and as a progressive web app, all from a single code base.

Just over 3 years ago, he was presented with a position that provided him a chance to learn JavaScript. So, he jumped at the opportunity and has never looked back. Douglas is developing apps using Ionic and Angular Frameworks, Google Cloud Platform, and Firebase. He is also forever committed to learning, and has specifically begun working with SVG, D3, TensorFlow and Actions on Google to bring animations, data visualizations, machine learning and voice interactions under his belt.

Douglas has worked in the IT industry for over 15 years, starting as a network technician, followed by positions working with business leaders as a software sales representative and as a IT services consultant. He understands how to communicate with business leaders, he knows how important their expectations are, and ultimately he knows how to deliver to them. His intrinsic interest in the sciences along with an ingrained desire to understand how things work have allowed him to keep at the forefront of the IT industry. He is a self-starter and fully autonomous; but he does enjoy the the company of peers and works well in any team. Douglas enjoys that feeling of success achieved upon implementing a complex solution that enables a client to truly make an impact in their market, and he has every intention to continue in that success.


He holds a B.S. Biology from Texas Tech University in Lubbock, Texas.
