About Me

Developing web applications as a frontend or fullstack engineer since 2017, I have worked on solo projects and large teams. The majority of my work has been in developing mobile first PWAs that have native functionality with the appropriate look & feel for the device used, utilizing one codebase, made possible with the Ionic Framework. I enjoy working on complex projects requiring state management, developing new feature sets, and eliminating bugs. The projects I have worked on followed the agile methodologies, and we worked on scrum teams. Those projects have exposed me to the most popular environments, SDKs, APIs, libraries, collaboration platforms, dev tools and ticketing systems. I am quite capable at creating my own design work, but I am most efficient at developing the code, based on a designer's wireframes. I prefer working with NGRX for state management and utilizing Nx for maintaining monorepos. If you feel that my background is fitting with your project needs, I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Work History

I graduated from Texas Tech University in 2000, and I immediately started working in the IT industry. My first career was at a rapidly growing ISP, where I moved up the career ladder of Technical Support, ultimately troubleshooting the network and colocated server issues of our Premier clients, nationwide. Later still, I delivered consultative sales of a full suite of Corporate IT services to Texas and Oklahoma Enterprise level businesses as an IT Services Consultant for a major printer manufacturer. A spectrum of roles within the IT industry and a well established set of software engineering skills has enabled me to get the job done right and on time, as a team player; and capable of effective communication with colleagues, management and clients.
